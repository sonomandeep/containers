# Authentication

Containers uses email and password authentication with mandatory email
verification.

## Sign up

Create an account with your name, email, and password. You will receive a
verification email to confirm ownership of the address.

## Verify email

The verification email contains a link. Click it to activate your account and
sign in.

## Sign in

Use your email and password to access the application. If your email is not
verified, you will be prompted to verify it first.
